Description

Raiders Camp is one of the biggest currenly unlocked worldmap random events. Alongside with Drug Lab is equally the most sought one.

The story of such places appearing on worldmap is grim: some prisoners located there tried to escape. They failed, before death though they menaged to lift a flare to warn all wastelanders nearby. When you and you team arrive on the event zone, prisoners are allready death - this means that place you just visited if full of angry, agressive raiders. Obviously somebody has to stop them!

Raiders Camp can be inhabited by: Highwaymans, Gangs members, Raiders, Rogues or Khans. Number of enemy is this location is quite big. While doing it alone is still menagable its advised to take a friend or two here. If not for shooting then at least to carry out the loot...

Encounter Version A

This Raiders camp seems to be placed in old and destoyed Gas Station. It is protected by barricade from used cars. The tent on the left has a 2 locked crates with explosives while main building on the right holds rest of goods. Its important to mention that once you are inside of the actual camp you can leave the map by going the hidden path leading to exit grind placed behind the gas station building. Almost every Raider is standing in the northern part of map. South not only is devoted out of npcs but also its out of any interesting lockers.

Encounter Version B

This camp seems to be some kind of factory .Just like other version this one is also protected by barricade of cars. The facility is placed on the cliff which seems to forify this position even more. The big building contains only two footlockers with random resurces spawn, while the smaller building on the right maintain an warehouse function. This is where you can find most of loot.

The whole area is crawling with NPC's.
