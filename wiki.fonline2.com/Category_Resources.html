Description

There are several resources in FOnline 2, used for the creation of guns, armors, ammunition, drugs and everything else you need. We can divide them into three groups: common resources, rare resources and tier 4 crafting components (aka ultra rare resources).

Common Resources

These are the main resources needed for all craftable items. They are quite easy to get.

Broc Flower

The plentiful flower that forms the base for the powder of healing.
Weight 0g
Base Price 1
Where to Find?
A man in Bluesuit gathering Broc Flowers. They appear in random encounters quite often.
Notes Used in creation of drugs...or for THIS and offcourse FOR THIS.

Cord

Trimming made of multiple strands of yarn attached together.
Weight 0g
Base Price 8
Where to Find?
While they sometimes appear in junk piles and such, the most efficient way is to browse NPC shopkeepers' wares. Here is nice stock of 11 found at one of Den's traders, worth less than a standard radio. What a bargain!.
Notes Used in creation of armors. Valuable and important resource.

Empty Hypodermic

This is an empty hypodermic.
Weight 0g
Base Price 2
Where to Find?
A woman scavenging Boxes.
Notes While scavenging for them is an option, NPC doctors usually have huge supplies of them in stock. Another option of getting them is just...waiting - each time you use a Stimpak or Super Stimpak there's a chance that you'll recover one.

Fire Gecko Pelt

This is the dried and cured hide of a Fire Gecko.
Weight 1600g
Base Price 350
Where to Find?
Cute yet deathly creature. Watch out for it fire!
Notes In order to skin gecko corpses you need the Gecko Skinning perk. Fire Gecko pelts can be used for crafting Hubbologist's Robes or sold for 350 caps in Modoc Tannery (only during global buyout event).

Firewood

A Pile of firewood and kindling.
Weight 3200g
Base Price 20
Where to Find?
Lord of evil gathering raw materials for Nazgul Stables. Or some random wastelander that went insane from jet overdose.
Notes In order to cut wood you can either use Hatchet or Primitive tool. Primitive tool brakes easily, so if you want to use it - take a few spares with you.

Flint

A stone used to sharpen weapons.
Weight 907g
Base Price 5
Where to Find?
A random man trying to find some flint. Its worth of note that Flint's sneak is around 300%. Those sneaky little rocks are hard to spot.
Notes They really are hard to spot, use zoom in and just try to adjust your eyesight to weirdly looking little rocks.

Fruit

A strange piece of fruit. No preservatives and no additional food coloring added.
Effect Cure few HP's
Weight 453g
Base Price 10
Where to Find?
Since all easy to spot fruit are already eaten, only the true fruity masters of camufluge survived. This poor wastelander finally found one after hours of searching

Wasteland encounters near trees
Shops
Notes Hard to spot, mostly used as an first product in chain of flamethrower ammo production.

Gamma Gulp Beer

A bottle of Gamma Gulp beer. It glows in the dark
Effect -1 to PE for 30 min
Weight 0g
Base Price 5
Where to Find?
Seriously, you are wondering where to get a beer?
Notes You can craft Rad-X with them. After drinking you'll get a left over bottle cap. When left on ground its really glows in the dark - Awesome.

Gecko Pelt

This is the dried and cured hide of a Gecko.
Weight 907g
Base Price 100
Where to Find?
Small group of your soon-to-be pelts
Notes In order to obtain skins from bodies you need Perk Gecko Skinning. Instead of using it to craft you can also sell this skin for 40 caps in Hub(talk to Traper Gorn) or for 100 caps in Madoc Tannery.

Golden Gecko Pelt

This is the dried and cured hide of a Golden Gecko.
Weight 1850g
Base Price 200
Where to Find?
Shoot. Skin. Sell. Spend everything on Iguana's.
Notes In order to obtain skins from bodies you need Perk Gecko Skinning. Instead of using it to craft you can also sell this skin for 80 caps in Hub(talk to Traper Gorn) or for 200 caps in Madoc Tannery.

Gunpowder

A Box with black powder.
Weight 500g
Base Price 50
Where to Find? 2x Into .
You`ll gain 20 exp in the process.
Notes Most basic and essential material for crafting any ballistic ammunition. You can craft it from mined minerals, or by dismantling bullets. How come no one has ever lost fingers while doing the latter - it is one of many wasteland mysteries.

Junk

A pile of junk parts. A little bit of everything.
Weight 2600g
Base Price 400
Where to Find?
A Hunter straight before its prey.
Notes Required by a lot of guns and ammunition. It's heavy but it's worth collecting anytime you notice a source while farming or doing any other business.

Metal Parts

Various metal based parts, with a creative mind at work coud be useful.
Weight 400g
Base Price 50
Where to Find? 2x Into .
You`ll gain 20 exp in the process.
Notes It is used nearly for everything: guns, ammo, armors, anything made of metal. You`ll use up a ton of them so gather metal parts whenever it is possible. Necropolis Vault guards are carrying a good amount of them, so raiding Necropolis for metal parts seems like a solid, yet tough option.

Microcircuits

Miscellaneous computer parts with various functions. A computer geek's dream.
Weight 453g
Base Price 100
Where to Find?
We're up all night to get lucky

Scavangeable computers are placed in many dungeons with Vault 15 being fine example.
Notes Mostly useful in creation of energy guns, but also required to craft some of the big guns, like Avenger Miniguns.

Minerals

A hard nature formation. Contains various useless and useful substances.
Weight 2000g
Base Price 10
Where to Find?
A minerals source.
Notes Boring grind, or a unique chance to roleplay a post-apocalyptic miner. Where's the fun in hitting the rock, if it doesn't try to hit you back? Find out for yourself!


Richest deposits can be found in caves.

Ore

A piece of natural mineral formations. Contains iron and its compounds. Very heavy.
Weight 4000g
Base Price 100
Where to Find?
A ore source.
Notes Boring grind, or a unique chance to roleplay a post-apocalyptic miner. Where's the fun in hitting the rock, if it doesn't try to hit you back? Find out for yourself!


Richest deposits can be found in caves.

Roentgen Rum

A bottle of Roentgen rum. It glows in the dark
Effect -2 to PE for 30 min
Weight 0g
Base Price 5
Where to Find?
Seriously, you are wondering where to get a beer?
Notes Used in crafting of RadAway. If you put it onto the ground it will emit light in the night!

Rot Gut

A very strong liquor, a cleaning fluid, or fuel for your car, you decide.
Effect -2 to PE for 30 min
Weight 0g
Base Price 20
Exp for Crafting 40
Requirments to craft 5x1x
Crafts a bath of 5.
Notes Used in crafting of Molotov Coctails and Flamethrower ammo.

Scorpion Tail

The served tail of a radscorpion.
Weight 5071g
Base Price 10
Where to Find?
Small Familly of scorpion tails users. If Tandi wins through this ambush she`ll be able to lot tail from each of those scorpions.
Notes Poison Antidote can be made with those.

Tanned brahmin hide

Brahmin hide, the best you'll find anywhere in the wasteland, high quality, tanned material.
Weight 2870g
Base Price 100
Where to Find?
You can either play as a post-apocaliptic shephard or kill&skin. Tandi this time choosed the first option, but tomorrow she might need a new armor, and the wasteland is harsh...
Notes Very important resource, necessary to craft armors. Just don't ask how the hell brahmin can walk having their hides already tanned. It is one of those wasteland mysteries.

Tobacco

Some sort of plant leaves, looks like tobacco.
Weight 0g
Base Price 30
Where to Find?
Women in bluesuit on quest to feed her urge. Smokers ain't got easy in world when they have to make their own smokes.
Notes Only a few wastelanders use tabacco to produce cigarettes, in most cases it is abused for decorative purposes.

Torn Paper

Part of paper witch by the looks have been torn off somewhere.
Weight 0g
Base Price 1
Where to Find?
Enclave Patrolmen looking for Torn Papers.
Notes Only an Enclave soldier woud be silly enough to scavange for it. Cigarattes are easily obtainable in multiple other ways, and crafting them is the slowest possibility.

Tube

Tube has been always looking like a tube. Steel and hollow inside.
Weight 1200g
Base Price 5
Where to Find?
BoS Paladin wants to craft new minigun since the old one was stolen by some crazy wastelander. He will need at least 6 tubes for it.
Notes Tubes are used mostly in production of guns.

Xander Root

The rare root that gives healing properties to the powder of healing.
Weight 1200g
Base Price 2
Where to Find?
The game's appearance of a Xander Root. This poor bluesuit doesn't know that majority of wastelanders will kill him for hes jumpsuit.
Notes Used in creation of drugs.

Rare Resources

Base Price 600

Expensive but safe.
Notes Price in San Francisco - 1000 caps
Price in Vault City - 1500 caps

Chemical Components

Various chemical components of unknow origin, most probably ingredients for drugs, medicine.

Cheap but risky.

Expensive but safe.
Notes Price in San Francisco - 200 caps
Price in The Hub - 300 caps

High Grade Steel

The usual stainless high durability steel, though this metal seems to be of a much higher quality.

Where to Find?
Cheap but risky.

Expensive but safe.
Notes Price in Redding - 1000 caps
Price at Gun Runners - 1500 caps

Kevlar Polymer

Synthetic fiber of a kind, very high strength, used in composite materials.

Base Price 300
Where to Find?
Cheap but risky.

Expensive but risky(ugh something went wrong here).
Notes Price in Gecko - 500 caps
Price in NCR - 750 caps

Refined Uranium Ore
