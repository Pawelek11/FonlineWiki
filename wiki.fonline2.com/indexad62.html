Description

„
An abandoned warehouse like hundreds of its like in the Boneyard.
”

Warehouse is small sized open PvE location. Getting through it usually takes only up to 10 minutes so if you're short on time and yet want to have some action this is a good choice. Beware! Deathclaws are really strong and while it is possible to beat this place alone it definitively won't be easy.

The Warehouse itself was initially home for gang called Rippers. Just before events of Fallout 1 they got chased away by herd of Deathclaws. We are safe to assume similar thing happens in FOnline 2 as just like in Fallout you can meet some Deathclaws on surface and the powerful Mother Deathclaw down in the basement

Enemies

1. Deathclaws

Ultimate machines of doom. This big creature can attack you only if they get close but they are faster than average wastelander, tougher than anything else on the wasteland and posses ability to deal a lot of damage very quickly. Each killed Deathclaw is awarded by 900 experience points. Exception to this are smaller ones - those are worth only 350 and the Mother Deathclaw which is worth whooping 5k experience points. Deathclaws can't be looted.

Playthrough

If you go in there, you will have the pleasant or unpleasant surprise to see that its full of dangerous Deathclaw which only seek to rip you apart. Center building is the only one worthy of note as that's the place where all loot is located and where you can find stairs leading inside.

In the basement you will find Mother Deathclaw accompanied with few smaller ones. Kill it, receive 5k experience points reward and then you can safely loot bodies scattered on the floor.

Note: While its possible to do this place alone (You need a Big Gun build with a lot of toughness and healing items.) its best to take some friend with you on this expedition.

Points of Interest

Warehouse

  • 1 - Stairs to the underground level.
  • 2 - Ladder to the underground level(alternative entry point).
  • c - Containers (No lockpick needed).

There are much more containers in this area but only ones in the main building are capable of spawning loot.

Lair

  • 1 - Entrance.
  • 2 - Perfect defense spot(Only two Deathclaw at once, can attack you if you're standing at this place, it is recommended to move there as soon as you enter this location, then for example Flamer or Grenades could become excellent weapons choices.)
  • 3 - Entrance from ladder.
  • c - Containers.

Trivia

Before introduction of Glow (and implants) this was one favorite place for PKs to lurk. Even now sometimes (especially at evening) you might find players there waiting for you in the shadows with their big guns.
