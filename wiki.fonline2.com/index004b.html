Special encounters are legacy coming from Fallout 1 & 2 where player during his journey could encounter weird, sometimes funny, sometimes profitable, sometimes dangerous special encounters. In FOnline 2 special encounters are working very similar to the ones from old games. So far in game there are exactly 13 special encounters.

Note: Please note that there is no way to increase your chance of finding this encounters. You just have to travel on world map and one day, one hour you will find them.

Note: You can't find twice same special encounter on same character if you already got it, till (probably) you find all 13.
