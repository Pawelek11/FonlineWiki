Lets play some tragic!

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
This is how all professionial Magic players looks like...i mean Tragic.

Description

This professional Tragic player will play a game of Tragic with you for every Tragic deck that you bring him. You can find "Rare" and "Regular" Tragic decks on both low and high tier NPCs ("farming" is usually the fastest way to obtain them, although sometimes you can find them in stores as well). Whether you win or lose he will take away your deck. That greedy, greedy, half-disintegrated little ghoul!

Note: Winning or losing is totally random. You can't improve your chances of winning by having high Luck or investing skill points into your Gambling skill.

Rewards

If you lose: 200 experience.
If you win: 1000 caps and experience.
