See the bottom of this page for a list of all close combat weapons a player can use in FOnline 2. Some of those weapons also fits in throwable category as you can thrown them.

While checking damage for close combat weapons remember that those weapon also use bonus damage from "Meele Damage" for example char with meele damage 10 will be dealing 30-50 damage with Eli's Knife.

List of Tier 0 Close Combat Weapons

Those weapons are mostly being used by NPCs or are being used as collectibles items with no real combat value.

Boxing Gloves

A pair of boxing gloves that smell faintly of sweat. They look like they have seen a lot of use: many old blood stains still remain.
Damage 1 - 1
Punch AP: 3
Range 1
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk Knockback (Only in thrust mode).
Strength required N/A
Weight 2267 grams
Base price 250 caps
Where to find?: Very rare dungeon/cave spawn.
Notes Those Gloves are really, rare due to some kind of spawning bug as the Plated one seems to be spawning way more often. The Knockback perk won't work as it's requiring thrust mode, which this weapon doesn't have - yep that' a bug.

Brass Knuckles

Hardened knuckle grip that is actually made out of steel. They protect your hand, and do more damage, in unarmed combat. Min ST: 1.
Damage 9 - 19
Punch AP: 3
Range 1
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 1
Weight 453 grams
Base price 40 caps
Exp for Crafting 30
Blueprint No
Requirments to craft: 2x
Notes Hey! What an useless weapon!

Club

A military or police baton. Heavy wood. Min ST: 3.
Damage 20 - 36
Swing AP: 4

Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 3
Weight 1360 grams

Exp for Crafting 75
Blueprint No
Requirments to craft: 1x1x

Notes If Police in Hub would be using this, those dirty wastelanders with Miniguns and Plasma Rifles would never dare to cause any trouble within the city at all!
