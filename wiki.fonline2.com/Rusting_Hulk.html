Description

„
You encounter: An old hulk rusting in the middle of a mountain road.
”

In this encounter you find dead's man body guarded by a single dog. The biggest object in this encounter is a abandoned giant vehicle. While vehicle cannot be taken (despite description telling that except for its tanks being empty its in the working conditions) the body can be searched. If you decide to defile last resting place of that man you will find: Combat Knife, 34x Shotgun Shells, Sawed-Off Shotgun and a... Music Box! When you select a "use" option on Music Box it will play an short but nice melody! Such a great and unique loot! Song however, is near inaudible, so crank that speaker up and place a megaphone besides that. You'll need it.

If you hover your mouse over dog the following text will display: "You see an Australian cattle dog. It has a dirty scarf around its neck, and seems to be guarding the corpse nearby." If instead you will hover around vehicle you can read: "This rusting old hulk is in surprisingly good condition. It could probably even run, if it hadn't been pumped dry."

Obviously, the whole special encounter is a reference to "Max Mad" the classic Australian post-apocalypse series of movies. Nevertheless many serious questions remains: A) How does dog is still alive? B) How does, you can't drive the car despite its description telling otherwise, C) Whats an Australian's dead body does on east coast of North America? Again, another mystery the NPCs will take to their graves.
