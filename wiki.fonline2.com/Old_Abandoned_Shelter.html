Description

Old Abandoned Shelter
A small, pre-war fallout shelter.
Price 30 000 caps
Seller Name & Location Kathleen in Boneyard
Base Size Medium
Amount of Containers 10
Parking Places 8
Other Notes -

Abandoned Shelter is one of the cheapest bases in the game. It costs only 30k and offer quite a big room for placing your stuff above plus obviously the abandoned shelter itself containing many places to put your stuff like boxes and lockers. Its good choice for first base as it can serve as a main one without needing of crafting extra containers. You are able to park about 8 cars there.
