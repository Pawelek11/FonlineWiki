Lavenders Flowers for Miss Binderson.

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Binderson's Bookstore.

Description

Miss Binderson is looking for any remaining book copies of "The Lavender Flower". She hates that book, and simply wants to erase it from our timeline. Since this is a very educational reference, you will get some experience in the process.

Reward

  • 500 experience points for each brought book.
