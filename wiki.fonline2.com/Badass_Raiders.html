Badass Raiders

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

This is tougher version of Raiders they are worth a little more experience points per kill and are having more hit points. They are very similar to regular ones, even in game they are called exactly the same. Wiki though need a distinction in naming and thus we called them Badass (Although they don't quite live up to their names.)

Other Information

Location: Arena between Broken Hills and Shady Sands
Weaponry: 10mm SMG, Grease Gun, Desert Eagle, Hunting Rifle, Assault Rifle
Extra Loot: 0-28 Caps per body, Stimpaks and other minor loot.
Difficulty: Average.
