Bootleggers

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

Bootleggers are a bunch of bandits focused on producing various kind of booze. They are aggresive and will shoot on sight protecting their supplies. How they are expecting to sell all their booze if they keep killing potencial client? The answer is simple - they will just drink the good itself and take all the cash from robbed "customers".

Other Informations

Location: Northern arena of the map, between New Reno and Den.
Weaponry: Hunting Rifles and such.
Extra Loot: Drinks! (And caps but caps are boring compared to drinks.)
Difficulty: Easy.
