Description

„
"The Brotherhood of Steel has many outposts in various areas throughout the entire Western Coast. One could name The San Francisco's Outpost as the most important one, as this town is very populated and posess some seriously advanced technology which must be monitored by BoS. Because of approaching threat from the Enclave, the Brotherhood of Steel decided to back up The San Francisco's Outpost with more supplies and troops. A Hummer full of gear protected by several Paladins has been dispatched from Lost Hills Bunker to the town. Unfortunately, convoy was detected by Super Mutants from Mariposa, and they decided to try to seize control over it. Right now, The strongest Mariposa's Super Mutants are invading the town, breaking through its defense lines one after another. Local Commander of Brotherhood of Steel, Scribe Matthew being in very dire situation resort to last desperate attempt of saving the town, he decides to send a radio call for help to everyone around that could possible hear him and aid the soon to be pillaged town. Matthew ask for assistance and offers a great sum of caps for everyone that will save the city.
”

This event is yet not finished and not much is being known about it. Few people were ably to take part in it, by taking part in closed beta tests, which results in giving us few screens. From the description in the changelog mentioning this event we get to know that reward is probably being consisted of caps. The basic sum of them will be decreased by every BoS solider killed action. It is told that event will last 20 minutes. It is probably one of the most epic events that will be available on FOnline2 however the exact date of its unlocking in the game is still unknown and currently there is no way to take part in it.
