Vault Dweller's Jumpsuit

Vault jumpsuits are the regulation clothing carried by the denizens of Vault-tec vaults. This one was once in possession of the famous Vault Dweller, who seemingly made several adjustments, making it more resistant and exceptionally durable.
AC bonus 5
Damage type Threshold Resistance

Laser 0 25
Fire 0 10

Weight 4535 grams
Base Price 90 caps
Where to find? * Guardian Of Forever
Notes Rare clothing that will for sure draw attention to your character, from other players and any Lady you manage to find.
