Robins briefcase

A metal briefcase containing some valuable items, that were robbed from a caravan to Shady Sans.
Usage Quest
Base Price 1 caps
Weight 0 grams
Where to Get? You will get it from Robin after you killed caravaneers.
Notes Can't be opened. Looks like no Bozars inside if your reputation with Gun Runners is 1000 or more.
