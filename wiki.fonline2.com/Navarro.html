Description

Note: Due to Enclave faction being blocked in session 3 due to rework, this place can't be accessed. One day though faction will be reopened and Navarro Camp will return to it's place on FOnline2 world!


„
A military base and a Vertibird refueling station, Navarro is one of the primary installations in the mainland of the former United States, of which the Enclave proclaims to be the sole legal successor.
”

To be able to see this place you have to be member of Enclave. Base is totally empty. There is no interaction with NPCs, no quests, nothing interesting for players at all except for working workbenches.
