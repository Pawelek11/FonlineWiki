Description

„
You encounter: A blue police box that seems to be out of its place...and time.
”

A sand. Endless sea of boring sand with nothing alive except for Floaters, Fire Gecko, Angry Super mutants and mean wastelanders. Once upon your journey maybe, just maybe you will find something different. You will see an unusual, rather large light blue police call box - the ones that were used on Great Britain in the era before mobile phones. If you approach the box, you will notice spinning light and box itself will disappear leaving a single motion sensor.
