Description

„
You encounter: As you approach the smoking ruins, you can see bodies piling up near the entrance.
”

This is the most disappointing special encounter in the game. You get nothing. You fight against nothing. You can't do anything there. So you enter the location. You can observe sealed bunker, some crates and a lot brotherhood trooper's bodies. Very boring. There is 8 dead bodies in total (3 dead Paladins each of them is on -21/70 hp, 5 Brotherhood Knight's 3 of them with -21/103 hp and 2 with -21/70). There are 4 containers, usually empty although some people were reporting finding of such powerful items like rope, empty hypodermics and tools. 2 Containers are titled coast, 2 are having "water" in their description.

Doors to bunker cannot be opened. C4 won't work. Shooting with rockets won't work. Doors are impenetrable. Truly Trollish Encounter. On more positive note - this encounter is having its own second version, sometimes instead of this one you can find "A Caravan Camp set up in Ruins", which is much more interesting. But since you're reading this article you probably ended up in Smoking Ruins variant.
