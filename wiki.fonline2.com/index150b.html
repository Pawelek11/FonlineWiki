Description

Punks are yet another kind of Robbers, this one being distinguishable by special sets of hair style like Mohawks. They will shoot players at sight so here goes away the "Punks are friendly" myth.

Other Informations

Location: San Franscisco and its territories.
Weaponry: Small Guns, Energy Guns, Grenades, Meele.
Extra Loot: Caps and regular minor loot.
Difficulty: Average
