Description

In mountains encounter sometimes you might encounter a map with cave. If you decide to enter one you'll find additional location tied to encounter. All caves are containing lootable bones container. In the bones you can find various items. All caves are also containing some kind of enemies.In addition to that caves are also spawning minerals and ore deposits from which wastelanders armed with Pickaxe may extract resources.On the side note in some caves you can find rock scattered through the floor - if you're rock collector this may be the biggest reason to go there. While exploring caves beware of other players that can easily ambush you.

Cave Types

There is only 6 different maps of caves:

  • This is small tunnel type cave.

  • Big cave with lots of room to manuver while clearing it.

  • Medium sized cave with long entry tunnel.

  • Biggest cave map in FOnline 2. I personally hate them since searching through it is tiresome.

  • Almost as big as cave type 4. This one is also very tiresome to browse.

  • My favorite cave map in FOnline 2. On the screen in place where Tandi stands there is a small choke point allowing defending player to easily beat the cave critters, as only one monster at the time can attack player standing there.

Ore & Minerals Deposit

Every cave will spawn some resources deposits. A standard pickaxe is required for mining them. The possesing of resources from cave deposits is usually much better idea than using the non-caves ones.
