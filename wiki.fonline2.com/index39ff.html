Pancor Jackhammer

The Jackhammer, despite its name, is an easy to control shotgun, even when fired on full automatic. The popular bullpup design, which places the magazine behind the trigger, makes the weapon well balanced & easy to control. Min ST: 5.
Damage 23 - 35
Single AP: 4
Burst AP: 5, 5 bullets per burst
Range 18(Same for burst)
Damage type Normal
Ammo or
Ammo capacity 15
Weapon Perk Accurate
Strength required 5
Item Durability 100
Weight 5443 grams
Base price 5500 caps
Exp for crafting 620
Blueprint No information yet
Requirments to craft: THIS WEAPONS IS LOCKED AND CAN'T BE CRAFTED.
Notes This is supposed to be top shotgun type gun. For now though it's damage is worse than H&K CAWS. This weapon is currently locked and await rebalance. It's still possible to find it on the wasteland in hand of some players though. As every Shotgun it has a chance to knockdown the target and it deals full burst damage on any range (AKA "range 1-hex").
