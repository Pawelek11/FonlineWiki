See the bottom of this page for a list of all throwable weapons a player can use in FOnline 2. Some of those weapons also fits in close combat category as you can bash with them.

The range displayed in following item stats is their max range. If yours strength is low you might not be able to throw them this far.

List of Tier 0 Throwable Weapons

Plant Spike

An organic seed-spike that the carnivorous plants spit at potential food or seed carriers.
Damage 1 - 2(punching)
1 - 3(throwing)
Throw AP: 4
Punch AP: 3
Range 8(When Throwing)
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk None
Strength required N/A
Weight 0 grams
Base price 0 caps
Where to Find? NPCs like Raiders or such drops it.
Notes I have no idea what this is doing in game.

Rock

It's a rock. The Granite-Inc. model is an upgraded version. Min ST: 1.
Damage 5 - 9(In both modes)
Throw AP: 4
Punch AP: 2
Range 15(When Throwing)
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk None
Strength required 1
Weight 0 grams
Base price 0 caps
Where to Find? Kids use it to scare off Wastelanders at Modoc Tannery.
Notes Rocks aren't the way. Use Ganja to make your opponents stonned.

Sharpened Pole

A wood pole sharpened at one end. Min ST: 4.
Damage 11 - 16(In both modes)
Throw AP: 6
Thrust AP: 4
Range 15(2 When Thrusting)
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk None
Strength required 4
Weight 1360 grams
Base price 5 caps
Where to Find? *Small Village
Notes Exclusively for role playing purposes or picking the giant stone head's nose.

Sharpened Spear

List of Tier 1 Throwable Weapons

Molotov Coctail

A home-made flammable grenade. Min ST: 3.
Damage 34 - 52
Throw AP: 5
Range 12
Damage type Explode
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 3
Weight 454 grams
Base price 50 caps
Exp for Crafting 75
Blueprint No
Requirments to craft: 5x
Crafts a batch of 10.
Throwing: 50%
Notes Not to usefull against tanks. Both in game and real life. Stil they shoud be good against random NPCs. An Hex shooting attack mode can be performed with this item. Also you can perform hex shooting with this item even when its hold in secondary hand slot.
When targeting with this item you will see Arena of Effect in form of red circle.

Throwing Knife

A knife, balanced specifically for throwing. Made of titanium, and laser sharpened. Min ST: 3.
Damage 25 - 40(When Throwing)
15-34(When Swinging)

Swing AP: 3
Range 16(When Throwing)
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 3
Weight 453 grams
Base price 100 caps
Exp for Crafting 60
Blueprint Yes

Crafts a batch of 10.
Throwing: 50%
Notes Many players instead of killing wastelanders would rather toy with them, crippling them or forcing them to drop a weapon. This is perfect tool for such dreadful people.

 


List of Tier 2 Throwable Weapons

Grenade (Frag)

A generic fragmentation grenade. Contains a small amount of high explosives, the container itself forming most of the damaging fragments. Explodes on contact. Min ST: 3.
Damage 80 - 100
Throw AP: 4
Range 15
Damage type Explode
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 3
Weight 453 grams
Base price 150 caps
Exp for Crafting 225
Blueprint Yes
Requirments to craft: 2x5x
Crafts a batch of 10.
Profession: Scientist 1
Notes Produces knockdown effect on target(50%). An Hex shooting attack mode can be performed with this item. Also you can perform hex shooting with this item even when its hold in secondary hand slot.
When targeting with this item you will see Arena of Effect in form of red circle.

 


List of Tier 3 Throwable Weapons

Grenade (Plasma)

A magnetically sealed plasma delivery unit, with detonating explosives. Creates a blast of superheated plasma on contact. Min ST: 4.
Damage 80 - 100
Throw AP: 4
Range 15
Damage type Plasma
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 4
Weight 453 grams
Base price 300 caps
Exp for Crafting 300
Blueprint Recipe
Requirments to craft: 4x2x1x
Crafts a batch of 10.
Profession: Scientist 3
Notes If you want to throw stuff at people choose this one. You probably still wont be able to kill them but at least they`ll have to use First Aid after first few. On the other hand grenades seems not terrible against NPCs in crowded spaces...Produces knockdown effect on target(25%). An Hex shooting attack mode can be performed with this item. Also you can perform hex shooting with this item even when its hold in secondary hand slot.
When targeting with this item you will see Arena of Effect in form of red circle.

 


List of Tier 4 Throwable Weapons

Greande (Pulse)

An electromagnetic pulse grenade, generating an intense magnetic field on detonation. Doesn't affect biological creatures. Contact fuze. Min ST: 4.
Damage 300 - 400
Throw AP: 4
Range 15
Damage type EMP
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 4
Weight 453 grams
Base price 250 caps
Where to Find? Rare loot in dungeons.
Notes A rare grenade that coud come handy in place filled with robots such as Glow or Sierra. An Hex shooting attack mode can be performed with this item. Also you can perform hex shooting with this item even when its hold in secondary hand slot.
When targeting with this item you will see Arena of Effect in form of red circle.

Signal Flare

A flare. Creates light for a short period of time. The paper is a little worn, but otherwise it is in good condition.
Damage 1 - 1
Throw AP: 2
Range 15
Damage type Normal
Weapon Perk Accurate
Weight 453 grams
Base price 35 caps for Craftable ones
60 caps for rare variants.
Requirments to craft: 2x1x1x
Science 30%
Exp for Crafting 125
Other Rare Variants Blue Green
Orange Red
White Yellow
When Lit: StandardBlue
Green Orange
Red White
Yellow
Where to find rare variants? Dungeons
Notes Mostly usable for decoration of base. Just lit it then drop on the ground(only rare color variant can be used for it). If you lit it then equip into hand slot you`re character will be surrounded by color aura.

The other important function of flare is creating an visible for everyone location on worldmap. Activate Flare then drop in on the ground. A Small Red Dot shoud appear on worldmap for other wastelanders as long as flare remains active (8 minutes). You can't active flare once location is closed with happens after 15 minutes.
