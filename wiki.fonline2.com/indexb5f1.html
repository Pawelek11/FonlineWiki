Description

„
This town, built on the remains of Klamath Falls, is the main source of gecko hides in the Core region.
”

The small town of Klamath is almost a ghost town. Town Control system is the only thing which motivates player to even travel to this shit hole. Players new to FOnline2 can do a little task for Aldo, but other than that this town feels very, very empty. Klamath is mostly famous for its bathhouse.

Battling in Klamath is unique compared to other towns. The map is huge, and it's hard to find any place with good cover, due to that Klamath is considered as a Snipers' favorite playground.

As mentioned earlier - othen that coming for town control there isn't really anythingh to do here for players. Maybe, just maybe you can come here to check the Vic's offer which while isn't that bad is just not worth the travel distance that you usually have to make before getting to this town.

So here it is, Klamath is plain, boring, empty. Probably the worst town in whole FOnline 2 universe. If you journeyed here, i advise you to change walking direction and go back into civilization as fast as you can.

Points of interest

Klamath

  • 1 - Vic, the NPC trader.
  • 2 - Maida Buckner. A minor NPC trader. She trades mostly in Nuka Colas and Cigarettes. She will also buy gecko hides from you (a repeatable quest).
  • 3 - Town Control box.
  • 4 - Aldo. He needs 5 bottles of "medicine" (rotgut, beer or booze will do just fine).
  • 5 - The workbench location.
  • 6 - Sajag. Just like Maida Buckner, he is just another minor trader.
  • 7 - The workbench location.
  • 8 - Smiley. If you want to initiate Town Control, this is the man you need to talk to.
  • 9 - Whiskey Bob. Buy him a drink and he'll give you one of those runaround quests.

The quest was removed from Klamath with note: "to fix" then forgotten. One day he might return and then Klamath will become a little more useful town.
