Enclave Platoon

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

Enclave Platoons are one of the most mean and nasty encounters availaible in the whole game. Those mobs are usually hunted only for the challange as while reward for killing them is quite big in both terms of experience and stuff, they are just that hard to beat.

Note: Nope, you can't encounter them in current session. They are blocked as Enclave faction is being reworked.

Other Informations

Location: On the western coast, near Navarro.
Weaponry: Plasma Rifles, Avenger Miniguns and other high tier weaponry.
Extra Loot: All sort of difrent accessories including plenty of caps.
Difficulty: Extremaly hard, almost impossible to beat this encounter solo.
