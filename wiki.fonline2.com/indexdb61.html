Descriptions

Another bunch of raiders. This kind is called Highwaymans because they mostly specialise in ambushing travellers. Quite dangerous and armed, you better put the end to their raiding every time you encounter a group of them.

Other Informations

Location: Practically everywhere.
Weaponry: Small Guns and meele weapons.
Extra Loot: Caps, stimpaks and other minor loot.
Difficulty: 4/6 (A little tougher than avarage but not really.)
