Description

 
Enclave sailed here using a barge.

"Enclave supply arrived in San Francisco docks. Time until departure: xx:xx"

Enclave is on with its offensive to the East. San Francisco already obeyed under heavy boots of Enclave Troopers. SF Docs is a perfect place to reinforce nearby ongoing operations by highly needed to be restocked supplies. Every single man who is against brutal Enclave invasion threatening order and lives of humble wastelanders should try to put on some resist. ...Of course such situation is also a very tempting opportunity for some nearby scums, smelling high rewards and a fresh blood possible to spill.

Enemies

They are using the most powerful weapons in the game. A Enclave trooper with a Bozar or Pulse Rifle is nothing weird during duration of this event. Obviously this weapons aren't lootable just the same with advanced power armors.

Tactic

 
As always in this game a real thread lies in other players not NPCs. Some happy PK`s with fresh loot.

Enclave guards are tough opponents, there is random number of guards(About +/- 12) armed in big guns and energy guns. Some of them might be armored in Advanced Power Armors other carry Enclave Armors. Guards aren't lootable. In order to deal with them it is advised to bring at least 3-4 players heavy armed and drugged.

The best spawning point is either Downtown and processing to docks via north point or waiting for event in the tanker and then attacking from Tanker. Be aware that other players might want to catch your group while fighting NPCs. Be on guard.
