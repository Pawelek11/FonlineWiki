NCR Combat Armor Mark II

High tech armor, made out of advanced defensive polymers. Mass produced version of Ranger Armor offers more ballanced protection.
AC bonus 25
Damage type Threshold Resistance
Normal 6 40
Laser 9 50
Fire 5 35
Plasma 5 30
Explode 9 45
Electrical 2 25
Anticrtical 5%
Durability 200%
Weight 11339 grams
Base Price 8000 caps
Where to find? * Quartermaster in Shady Sands will paint Combat Armor MK II for you if you're member of NCR Army.
Notes Its the same armor as regular combat armor mk II except for it's color.
