Car Paint

Car paint. Use it on your vehicle to apply this style.
Usage Change's Vehicle skin.
Base Price 12000 caps
Weight 0 grams
Where to Get? Mia Townsed in Hub sells them.
Notes Click on this item graphic to get to it's item full article page where you find more informations.

Description

It was hot September evening. Everyone has been on vacation, doing nothing, resting and in general doing whatever normal human being is doing in the last month of summer. Everyone? Nope. The FOnline2 community was working ultra hard during those hot summer days!

On September 17 a new thread has been made on FOnline2 forum in Task subsection. The Taskmaster - Wesan asked community to create as much skins as they can in order to bring much more variety to the game vehicles. No longer players had to deal with the issue of not knowing which car belong to who inside their gang bases. Not so long ago I remember when member coming from one of most famous Russian gangs has been speaking to the other during Ares PvP. "Mate u just took my Police Car, check up the trunk and give it back please I really need that electronic dildo!"

Through the next weeks a lot of skins has been created. You can check them up in this thread: http://forum.fonline2.com/index.php?topic=15179.0

About Paints

You can't find paints in the dungeons, the only way to acquire one is to buy it.You know? Like real life. You can buy it either from other players or you can check up the Hub's Oldtown NPC called Mia Townsend that lives in Tattoo shop. Remember, she does NOT sell tattoos. To buy a paint from that NPC just simply select barter option, remember that she will only accept bottle caps.

A typical set of cars. Okay maybe not so typical, but a nice set indeed, right?

Once you acquired paint just use it on your car (you need to have keys to that car, or be the person who mounted an electronic lock on it). You can then change the skin by using new paint, but every paint can has only one charge - so it can be used only once before it disappear.

Currently there is 90 different skins, some of them (2 Motorbikes and a Highwayman) can be only found in game files but not in actual game itself reducing that number to still very impressive number of 87 skins total.

Cockroach doesn't have any skins yet.
