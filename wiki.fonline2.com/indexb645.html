Description

Blueprints are consumable items that are necessary in crafting of armors, guns and drugs. In the beginning of a game season majority of items were blocked (except for tier 0 - the most basic items like 10mm Pistols or Flamers). To craft them the player has to find blueprints for them. When enough people find a blueprint for the same item, the item becomes unblocked and available to craft for everyone with needed professions and materials.

Blueprints also allows you to craft items with a crafting bonus (a possible random bonus to an item that can for example increase damage of the item or it's durability.) So its advised to collect them and consume even later in the season when every piece of equipment is already unlocked.

Blueprint Hunting

Finding a blueprint is a daunting challenge because they are very rare. But that shouldn't stop you from searching for them though, you can get them in:

  • Dungeons
  • Caves
  • Lockers scattered through random encounters

You can also try to buy them from other players! Even though a blueprint is destroyed after consuming (so there is only one character per one blueprint) some people are likely to find two copies, which they won't be needing. If you find them - all that's left is to take it from their hands either by bartering or by using some less unpleasant methods (or maybe pleasant, it's your call.)

Throwing Knives Blueprints?

Yep, you can find some seemingly useless blueprints like the ones for Buffout, Rad-X, RadAway, Throwing Knives and so on. They used to be useful when those items were still locked by the Global Tech feature and only people with blueprints were allowed to craft them. Nowadays such blueprints can be used in one of mini quests as a NPC's food.

Ok i get it but...Psycho Blueprints?

Yes, they are in the game, and sometimes you can find them. Yes there are blueprints for Psycho which is not craftable. Yep this is what we call a in-game-bug son.

Recipes

A Recipe is special kind of Blueprint. While normal blueprints once consumed will grant if effect permanently, recipe is different. Recipe is a crafting component necessary to craft some more advanced items. Each recipe can be used to craft only one item. They can be found in many places, scattered across the world of FOnline2.

Global Tech Tracker

To find out which items are already unblocked and don't require a blueprint to craft you should head to this thread: [Tech Tracker]

Remember that if items are already unblocked and you don't possess a blueprint for it you won't be able to get crafting bonuses on your freshly manufactured itmes. This doesn't apply to most basic items. There are no blueprints for items like the 10mm Pistol and you always have a chance to get a crafting bonus while making them.
