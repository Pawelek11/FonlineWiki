Description

„
"There are rumors about, that there is something suspicious happening in the Cathedral...10:00"
”


„
"It's quite obvious that sooner or later a great battle over setting clear who should dominate the entire Western Coast of North America will take place. There are two major players in this region. The Brotherhood of Steel, a pre-war, technology hoarding paramilitary organization which is slowly transforming from remnants of USA army into some kind of religious cult will have to fight the other big technology hoarder - Mysterious organization which calls themselves The Enclave. Both factions seems to be about equal in power, as while the Enclave control more superior pre-war technology, their army is smaller than the BoS faction. This is a great problem for Enclave's Leaders, because if they engage in battle their victory is not as certain as they would wish it to be (and as they nonstop proclaim).

Therefore before engaging into total battle over supremacy Enclave will try to cut down the number of BoS's Paladins in a very nefarious manner: Enclave is going to manipulate Supermutants into attacking the BoS bases. The meeting point is located in front of one of Unity Strongholds, at Cathedral in ruins of LA. Enclave has delivered to the mutants few pieces of their powerful technology. A insanely powerful energy weapon was shipped to the Super Mutants. Enclave knows that Unity will wants more of such technology, and while first one was a gift, the next shipments will have their price which is... 20 Paladin's heads in return for each Pulse Rifle.

Right now Enclave is delivering the cargo. If only there would be gang brave enough to storm the Cathedral, eliminate both Supermutants and Enclave Troopers and then seize the weapons for them own, before both Unity and Enclave realize they are being robbed..."
”

Note: Made out lore on spot is made up. But i hope it makes at least some sense right?

Gameplay

So this particular event is happening very rarely. You should consider yourself extremely lucky if you ever had been on one of those. When event is being activated a Vehicle guarded by several Enclave Guards will appear in front of Cathedral. Players will have to beat the Enclave Forces AND few supermutants that are also being spawned there, not to mention other Unity representative, that while seems not threatening in fact is having an Gatling laser in his pocket (Surprise motherlover!) When no enemy NPC is being alive players might loot the Scout's trunk which is having some very interesting content. This task seems quite heavy and i would recommend at least 8 players team minimal to take on this challenge.

As usual with this kind of events even if you deal with NPC's you still have to worry about other gangs that might attack you while trying to reclaim the loot. Beware!

Enemy Forces

It looks like players will have to eliminate:

  • 5 Supermutants (3x Rocket Launcher, 2x Laser Rifle with range upgrade)
  • 4 Enclave Soldier (3 of them in APA one in Enclave Armor, they are armed with Bozar, Pulse Rifle, Rocket Launcher and Gauss Rifle)
  • One Robbed Guy from Unity (At start he seems to have only Laser Pistol, but quickly after he will use Gatling Laser - beware!)

Okay, that was the easy part the additional obstacles are

  • Every PvP team that is online and is not currently allied to you, because you can be sure as hell that if you're are going there, they will go as well.
  • Blacksuit looters.
  • Musashi (Main player threat) trying to ninja loot, scout's trunk while you fight with NPCs. Suggested methods are to focus fire him when he is on the field. All characters names of his starts with "M"

Good news is that while NPCs aren't lootable, if you manage to win against other PvP teams you will surely loot some nice, nice things from them and will be able to show some cool screens in forum thread.

Rewards

The rewards might vary, but so far every Scout was having the Pulse Rifle.

  • 1x Enclave Armor.
  • 1x Hypo.
  • 16x Jet.
  • 1x Laser Pistol.
  • 6x EMP Grenades.
  • 1x Pulse Rifle.
