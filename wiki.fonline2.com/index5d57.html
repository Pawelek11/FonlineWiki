Description

„
A mysterious entrance hidden in the forested hills of Klamath. Rumor has it, that the dark maze of tunnels leads to an old facility from before the War.
”

Toxic Cave is the only instanced dungeon in FOnline2 (in other words it is dungeon that it's not visible for entire player-base like in other cases of FOnline2 dungeons). To find its location a player first has to find a Toxic Cave map. Since only the player reading the map knows where this dungeon is, the location is guaranteed to be PvP free (That is, unless you desire additional challenge to the game and bring along others.) This place is a multistory, tube-like dungeon which requires a minimum of two characters to complete in RT. (Or one in turn based. Dual logging is legal). Enemies here - Geckos are challenging but it's not crazy hard to kill them depending on your capabilities and preferences you can do this place in turn based mode or real time mode (Set to desired combat mode before reading the map.)

Note: This dungeon is unforgiving and will punish you for incompetence. Once you leave the location, either by death or an exit, any gear left behind will be lost.

Note: This dungeon can be done at a minimum of around 7 minutes. This will include completing the robotic maze in a time range of 20-30 seconds.

Note: Starting from recent update it is possible to find Implants or tier 4 crafting components in this location although chance is very, very slim. So far no one has reported successful finding one here.

Playthrough

After you enter prepare yourself for some toxic goo being all over that place. It damages you and also causes radiation so getting yourself a pare of shining rubber boots might be good idea. If you didn't bring any with you then try to find some in containers around this place. This place works just like classic Fallout 2 quest. Go in kill geckos, loot. Continue with your journey.

Initial Level

Once you enter you will be instantly attacked by 2 tough radiated gecko and one regular radiated gecko. Move bit forward and see ambush party of another radiated gecko + small one. Clear lockers on the right wing. In the right wing room there is another small gecko. When you're done looting this place go further. This was just a warm-up.

Inner Corridors

When you start moving into the level you will get attacked by 4 geckos: 2 small ones and 2 radiated ones, move further to corridor on very down. You will get attacked by small pack of Radiated Geckos. Now carefully go through the corridor trying to not step on green goo. As you travel this corridor you will get attacked by even more geckos. This time not only radiated one will show up but also Toxic Ones. They are a bit more dangerous so you can start paying attention now. Also remember that Toxic Geckos wield Toxic Gecko Skins, if you got the perk you can loot them. In middle of down corridor there is a sub corridor leading to a room with 4 lockers and now emptied nest (Gecko's that were there went out to attack you unprovoked so its only fair that you take their stuff now right?)

Ignore rest of this level unless you're looking for experience point as in the middle and top corridor there is nothing useful except for perhaps some more, angry geckos and get straight to the room with elevator, loot containers in this room, and go down, unlike Fallout 2 you won't have to repair the generator powering elevator or hack the elevator doors to go down, feels easy? Don't get cocky, FOnline2 prepared something way more troublesome just for you dear player.

Puzzle Level

Puzzle level! Favorite one amongst troll minded developers. Hated by everyone else. So this is where you need 2 people (or just one but very masochistic). One person will need to "talk" with the PC (ignore all options but "Enable Robot Maintenance." rest is just for trolling purposes) from this point the task of first person is to operate the robot and get him to the valve, in order to release toxic gases. In that time the other person must watch the robot movements and guide the operator. Have fun this is extremely irritating part of this dungeon!

When you're done with releasing the poisonous air, go to the other side of room. Naturally you will be attacked by few Toxic Gecko and one Mature Toxic Gecko, total of 3 enemies. Take care of them. Go further to the storage rooms.

Note: Alternatively if you are alone you can do it masochistic way. Click on Computer move robot hex or two, stand back from PC see how it went, return to PC, rinse and repeat. It will take ages but it's doable. Just definitely won't be fun.

Storage Room

Storage room is heavily guarded by big pack of Toxic Gecko. 3 Mature ones and 2 regular ones are rushing to you as soon as you spawn in. Kill them off quickly or they will overwhelm you if you don't finish them quickly enough as they are coming one by another. Watch out it's time for big fight again. Open big doors to even bigger room by your right side. There is 6 Mature Toxic Gecko there! Be very careful they can do some decent damage if you made yourself open to few of them attacking you at once. If you managed to kill them - the biggest storage room in this dungeon is yours to loot so have some fun. Once you're done it means its time for Last Level.

The Last Level

Surprisingly enough you won't get attacked here as soon as you spawn. Go through the doors. On the left side next to you there are some containers. On the right side you can see light of exit. There is like 3 mature Toxic Gecko guarding it though. Should be easy after all these big groups in last level. In the middle there is room with last containers in this dungeon. They are guarded by the biggest group of Toxic Geckos in them whole game, 7 maybe more, it would be a tough fight if not for the fact that you can just shoot them through the second partially opened doors. Or alternatively here's a quick tip: open doors, let them chase after you to start of this level, then close the first doors in the level right through their noses and shoot them through the broken window. Any way or another you should be able to kill them all. There will be few more in the small rooms but they most likely won't be too dangerous for you.

After every nook and cranny has been thoroughly searched and all that needs to be slain has been vanquished. It will mark the end of your adventure in the cave, should you attempt a second Toxic Cave immediately after the first, it is recommended that you cure yourself of radiation/poison through use of RadAway/Magical potions from local wizard or visiting a sorcerer.
