Description

 
Hunter's Camp
A couple of tents standing out in the open.
Price 30 000 caps
Seller Name & Location Jack in Junktown
Base Size Small
Amount of Containers 2
Parking Places 6
Other Notes Total cost of base is 30k and 5 caps as you need to buy a beer to NPC if you want to buy this base.

Hunter's camp is one of cheapest bases in the game. It has only 2 build in containers. On the map you can find two tents which makes is quite empty one. This is probably very bad choice for main base - it could be useful as an outpost type base for PvP player which needs a gathering point before going to Town Control or for dungeons.
