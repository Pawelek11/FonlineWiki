Gatling Laser

An H&K L30 Gatling Laser. Designed specifically for military use, these were in the prototype stage at the beginning of the War. Multiple barrels allow longer firing before overheating. Powered by Micro Fusion Cells. Min ST: 6.
Damage 60 - 80
Burst AP: 7(10 bullets per burst)
Range 40
Damage type Laser
Ammo
Ammo capacity 30
Weapon Perk Long Range
Gatling laser reduce target DR by 30%.
Strength reqiuired 6
Item Durability 200
Weight 10886 grams
Base price 7500
Where to find? * Find some crafting components and make it!
Exp for crafting 1000
Requirments to craft: 1x1x1x
4x8x
Profession: Gunsmith 3

Can only be crafted at Vault City Assemble Line (extra crafting cost 100k) or at Sierra Army Depot Assembly Line (free of charge).
Notes Ultimate Minigun. It deals huge amounts of damage. Requires energy weapon skills.
