Description

Events is taking place here.
Afterbattle.

"Drug transaction between Khans and Bishop Family is being made. Time until end: xx:xx"

Every few hours (I think that minimal cooldown time is 4 hours and maximal time - 9, but its just an assumption.) Khans make a deal with Bishop family in front of the Shark Club. A car and some guards spawn in front of the Casino. Allied team of players must confront heavy escort consisting of Khan Raiders (They are armed in random heavy-tier weaponry including Bazookas, Miniguns or Laser Rifles, and armored in either Leather Jackets or Metal Armors.), and whats could be even worse - other hostile players.

The additional problem is time. Event is open only for 10 min, after that Car and Guards disappear.

Enemies

They are using strong weapons likes Rocket Launchers, since they are NPCs they have no problem with focusing single players which makes it quite hard to heal yourself once you're getting hit from 2-3 rocket launchers at same time which altogether makes this event quite hard to bet for small number of players.

Tactics

Its best to spawn from world map on the Gas Station, then enter 'WarZone' area from there. Your group should take down raiders armed with bazookas first, as they pose the biggest threat. Minimal amount of players to do this event is 2 if they have multiple characters on World Map. Advised numbers of players is 3 or more. Take tough, experienced characters with big amount of hp and high Big guns skill.

Dealing with other players

If you encounter other players while doing event, try to take shelter in one of the buildings. Do not let your group get into crossfire or you`ll all die. If you`re the one ambushing it's usually easy to kill both NPCs and players busy fighting each other.
