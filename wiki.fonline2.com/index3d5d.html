Bring junks to Pete.

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

Pete needs 5 pieces of junk. There are many different ways of obtaining resources like this but the best way is to scavenge them in urban encounters (click on car wrecks, newspaper machines, boxes, barrels etc).

Rewards

  • 10 positive karma.
  • 750 experience.
  • 100 caps.
