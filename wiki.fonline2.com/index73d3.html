Description

Note: "This article will focus only on the quest part of the Cathedral run and will include spoilers. Check up Cathedral article in locations for more information about this awesome place. Did i mention spoilers? Well now that i did i can go super heavy on them since you are warned!


„
I have read a holodisc. It gave me instructions to perform a secret missions at the Cathedral.
”


„
Go to Cathedral. In order to increase your credibility among the members of the Children of Cathedral remember to fit into their dress code and get one of those purple robes. You will pretend to be an agent working for the Enclave. Break is our contact there. Find him. He knows, that we will send someone to meet with him. He has more instructions for you. He will most likely be in one of the upper floors. To get there you'll need a passphrase. It's "FEVer". Good luck Soldier!
”

To start this quest firstly you need to find Mission Briefing somewhere in the wasteland. It got slim chance to drop upon killing humanoid NPCs like Raiders or Peasants. Anything human-like will work as long as you have enough patience. Hunting for it might take a while. I'm just going assume that you found it... "In Disguise" is a hard quest that can be performed in Turn Based or Real Time. Real time is only doable if you got team of veteran players willing to join you, while turn based can be done with just one extra person (well it depends on many factors, for some players even 2-3 extra wastelanders won't be enough in TB while other people can try to do this place alone, all depends on how good stuff you're willing to risk and good your build is, and offcourse how experienced in turn based combat you are.)

Before departure:

  • Read the holodisk - depending on what game mode you have set the mission will take place in either Turn Based or Real Time mode. (Turn based is strongly suggested.)
  • Bring a lot of ammonition and super stims. Spare armor. Tools if your repair skill is low and you don't want to fail during it's repairing. Doctor Bag if you playing to fight in Turn based. Make sure your gun is in good condition and won't brake in middle of figth. Did i mention drugset?
  • Bring Robes with you. It's not super rare but also not very common spawn item. Try dungeons or Sierra Caves. You will get one eventually.
  • Make sure your friends are well prepared for the trip.
  • Make sure you have at least 40 minutes of free time before you start the quest.

Everything set? Go to Cathedral area. In front of doorway leading to Cathedral equip Robes and tell your companions to do the same. Ask them to tag you. Then make sure again that they tagged you. Then be sure to shout at the one guy that forgot to tag becuase reasons. Tell your team to stay put for next 5 minute and remind them to don't brake anything while you set things up...

He will outline your first job here and also gives you a quest holodisk. You can read it but unfortunely it's coded so all you will end up with is a long row of numbers.

Meeting with Rawryy The Console Room Cerberus

„
I talked to Break. He gave me another Holodisc. I Need to use the SAT Terminal upstairs to uplink the data to the Brotherhood of Steel.

Go there, then start the conservation.

Trip to Ghtang's Room

„
Break gave me a new task. I have to kill Tansien, the leader of the Children of Cathedral cult, to do this I need to get inside the Vault beath this building.
”

Proceed to highest level of the Cathedral building. This is the level where Ghtang is located. Before opening doors enable stealth and make sure you aren't running (unless you have combat running perk, otherwise you will exit stealth as soon as you start moving, becuase you can't run and stealth at the same time withouth perk) carefully reach the footlocker and loot only the quest item, don't risk taking anything else there or wasting more time at that room that's absolutely necessary. Trip's over and if you did it right Ghtang will never find out who took his key.

"A Visual Guide, how to get past the Ghtang."
Make sure to set: "always run" to off while doing this!

Bloody Mess

Wake up your team mates that probably felt asleep while waiting for you to obtain the necessary key. Go to underground levels. First level is full of centaurs and floaters, be sure to take anti-radiation drugs as if you won't they might weaken you (radiation bites can decrease amount of your action points for example), once you take care of them you will be fighting super mutants from now on. Among them there are few especially dangerous ones, the Stealth users, Nightkins. You can't detect them unless you are really close to them, and they on the other hand will shoot you from the distance making your life super miserable. Visit Cathedral page for information about enemies that you can find in this place. Basically, this is the shooting part of the quest. If you are alone - you probably won't be able to make it, if you are with just one team mate you have decent chance of clearing this place in turn based mode, if you are with entire gang - it's time for real time battle.

Once Tansien is dead - your job here is almost done. All that left is going to Break...

Rewards

  • 6000 caps from Brotherhood of Steel Paladin located in front of Shady Sands's Outpost.
  • 22000 experience points (10k for Killing Tansien assuming you did it alone, 6k for obtaining the key and also 6k for letting Break know about your success.)
  • Some Good Loot from Cathedral Vault (Totally Random, it is possible to find rare weapons and implants there, but chance is super slim.)
