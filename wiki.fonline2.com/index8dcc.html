Cave Camp is an rare base. A Cave camp map can be only found in dungeons such as Sierra or Glow or be bought from other players.

A cave camp is a base with two location. First one is outside, this is where car parking is located (it can hold up to 9 cars). There's also 2 tents with 5 containers: the tent on the left has 2 bookcases, while the one on the right is equipped with: 2 footlockers and a Box. There's also entrance to the cave itself. The cave contains 6 more containers - 5x lockers and one bookcase.

This base is unpopular choice amongst wastelanders. The first location is quite boring while inside of cave is a bit too cramped for most of wastelands needs.
