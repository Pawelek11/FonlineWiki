Description

„
The town was founded after the Great War by a soldier named Darkwater. Instead of the usual routine of settling in the ruins of the old one, he decided to start anew, and so the place was built mostly out of random pieces of junk and broken cars from the nearby scrapyard.
”

Junktown is one of the first cities that a player new to the world of FOnline2 will get to know. Its inhabitants offer many interesting quests, its security is very tight, and you can also find traders who can offer you some basic starting equipment. Areas surrounding this place are full of easy encounters, which makes this town a perfect starting point; that is until you get bored with it and decide to get a taste of the cruel wasteland.

Junktown Scouts

Junktown is the city managed by Junktown Scouts - A gang/faction ran by a bunch of buffoons (Players) It's members are a bit over privileged here. They can collect taxes from jail warden (officers only), they have access to private barracks (every person in the faction is having its own room), they have their very own building with a workbench (only officers can get inside) also the Junktown Scouts faction possess an NPC Shopkeeper located next to office, finally Junktown members can attend a patrol using a NPC located close to the east gate. A patrol is a feature working like a caravan route, except instead of one caravan leader helping, you got yourself a whole bunch of guards.

The Junktown Scouts project mission is to help newcomer, if you meet one in the city and need anything, ask them, while they don't have to help you, in most cases they will at least try to. Also its probably okay to join them just to be able to make a patrol, but then you will probably agree to not shoot newcomers or some similar sounding rules. Best way of joining the faction is probably through FOnline 2 forum or catching one of Junktown Scouts in-game.

Note: Unlike Shady Sands, in Junktown, scouts cannot shoot people at wish.

Points of interest

  • 1 - Caravan Packer, a local who can offer you the box lifting job.
  • 1a - Box Delivery Zone, drop boxes from Boxes lifting box quest here.
  • 2 - Joe Stinker. He can give you the epic quest of shoveling brahmin crap. You can also agree to bring him some water ("Get Waterbag to Joe quest").
  • 3 - Jail. If you break the rules, this will become your new home for some time.
  • 4 - Ron Winkley. He is the local Jail Warden. He can sell you items belonging to previously imprisoned players (accepts only caps).
  • 5 - The workbench location.
  • 6 - Mary Ann. You can bring her electronic parts for caps. She also asks wastelanders to bring record-o-matic to Benjami (quest name: "Broken Delivery").
  • 7 - Lilian, the main trader in Junktown.
  • 8 - Doc Zacharius. He has two small tasks for new players: 1) bring him 5 radscorpion tails and 2) bring him heart pills. He also barters, offers healing and sells antitoxins. Its the cheapest doctor in the game - he's services costs only 350 caps.
  • 9 - Melsak. An experienced tribal trapper who can teach you how to skin geckos. He is also offering brahmin hides if you agree to bring him the requested water bags (quest name: "My first tent").
  • 10 - Mike. The local Sheriff. Talk to him in order to start the "It's complicated" quest, involving him and Mary Ann.
  • 11 - Benjami. The "Skum Pit" bartender. Talk to him and ask about his life, and he will eventually ask you to convince Kylee, the whore, to stop her... bitching uh... I mean prostitution business. You can also barter with him for some sweet alcoholic beverages.
  • 12 - Jack. A hunter offering a hunter's camp base map for 30 000 caps (you'll need to buy him a beer first so bring 5 caps more).
  • 13 - Warren. He'll give you a quest called "I smell a rat". It's one of the repeatable farming quests.
  • 14 - Barber. She offers to change the hairstyle of any man in the wasteland with enough caps in his pocket.
  • 15 - Margaret. The owner of the local "Crash House" hotel. She has a problem with her fridge. First she'll ask you to go and nag Pete about it. After you return with Pete's dev-like "soon" answer, you can offer her to repair the fridge yourself. Make sure you use tools if your repair skill is low. If you succeed at fixing it, you'll get a repair book.
  • 16 - Kylee. She's the prostitute Benjami is concerned about.
  • 17 - Pete. The Junktown mechanic. He asks the player to bring him 5 pieces of junk. He'll also ask you to bring him a Super Tool Kit, offering to give you a free lesson in repairing (this gives you a +20% repair skill bonus, but your repair skill must be at low level; quest name: "Pete's Tools".
  • 18 - Jim. He can teach you the level 1 armorer profession.
  • 19 - Waterpipe. you can fill your empty water sacks here.
  • 20 - Loom. An NPC playing important role in "The glorious Jet supply storage" quest.
  • 21 - Yoz. So far it's just another useless NPC.
  • 22 - The Office. Junktown Scouts office. Only the Junktown Scouts leaders have access to this place.
  • 23 - Junktown Patrol Leader. You can go on patrols by speaking with this NPC. This feature is available only to Junktown Scouts.
  • 24 - Private Room. Only a member of Junktown Scouts can enter this area.
  • T - Player run shop.
