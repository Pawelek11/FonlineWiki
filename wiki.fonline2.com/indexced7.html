Description

This place is one of more interesting random worldmap encounters. What seems to be just another series of boring and empty ruins is in fact a masqerade of an underground base. The onground is practically empty - a lot of containers holding either very minor loot or no stuff at all. There are two entrances to the underground zone. First one is located to the north in a building. It's undefended and there are no mines there. The other one at south is mined and protected by a bunch of NPC's. Route from second entrance to the heart of this place is a bit shorter though. It's probably better choice to use north stairs.

If you enter from north location you will spawn at the center of an underground map (location 1). Your goal should be to go to the cooking lab of local bandits (2). Don't use the shortest route - its mined. Instead take a bit longer path through enemy groups killing them one after another. At some point, a far right enemy group might start shooting at you. You can either kill them or just run till you leave their range as they are positioned in place where they can't be reached. Basically like Orcs in Moria from Lord of the Rings. Go around the central passage until you reach backdoors. Open them and prepare for the toughest fight in this place. Plenty of NPC's!

If you manage to beat them, all there is left to do is to get through lock protected doors then loot main container which has plenty of drugs. Make sure to also loot shelves. You can kill Chemists or you can let them be. It's your call.

Main Container (2) looted? Awesome. It's time to leave this place before other players find you.

Points of interest

Onground

  • 1 - North Entrance, backdoor entrance.
  • 2 - South Entrance, main, protected and mined entrance.
  • 3 - Closed Doors, there's nothing interesting there.
  • 4 - Closed Doors, there's nothing interesting there.

Underground

  • 1 - North Entrance, backdoor entrance.
  • 2 - Main Fridge, it's protected by lock, plenty of stuff inside.
  • 3 - Protected Entrance, not only there are lot of mines here, but also doors are protected by a complex lock.
  • 4 - South Entrance, main entrance.
  • c(blue) - Container, a container with stuff.
  • c(pink) - Container, thats always empty.
