Needler Pistol

You suspect this Bringham needler pistol was once used for scientific field studies. It uses small hard-plastic hypodermic darts as ammo. Min ST: 3.
Damage 15 - 30
Single AP: 4
Range 24
Damage type Normal
Ammo or
Ammo capacity 10
Weapon Perk Penetrate
Strength required 3
Item Durability 100
Weight 1814 grams
Base price 2200 caps
Exp for crafting 150
Blueprint Yes
Requirments to craft: 1x2x1x1x
Profession: Gunsmith 2
Notes One of best SG Guns in game, it's quick and it has very good damage. Just remember to use AP ammo. A Perfect choice for medium range sniper or Pistolero. Merchants also like this more than 14mm Pistol, even though resource costs are identical.
