Description

According to Urban dictionary which is according to some guy called Tupac: "A thug is someone who is going through struggles, has gone through struggles, and continues to live day by day with nothing for them. That person is a thug. and the life they are living is the thug life. A thug is NOT a gangster. Look up gangster and gangsta. Not even CLOSE, my friend. "

Perhaps they are supposed to be a nice guys in real world, you can believe in it sure, some people believe in Giant Lizards controlling the USA, other in honest politicians, so why woudn't you believe in nice thugs? Homever in Fonline 2 they are ruthless agresive npcs that will shoot you on sight.

Other Informations

Location: All around the map, seriously they are almost everywhere.
Weaponry: Small guns and meele.
Extra Loot: Caps, collector items and whole lot of junks.
Difficulty: Avarage.
