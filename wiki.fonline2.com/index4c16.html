Brotherhood Dog Tag

This Dog Tag is engraved with Brotherhood of Steel symbols, the writing on the back reads: "Paladin Barett".
Usage Quest.
Base Price 20 caps
Weight 45 grams
Where to Get? *Small Village
Notes It's an object that will be usable in one of Brotherhood's quests. So far it can be delivered to Knight Matthes in Lost Hills Bunker for some small amount of experience.
