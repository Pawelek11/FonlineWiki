Description

 
Small House
A tin building sprouting out of the wasteland.
Price 40 000 caps
Seller Name & Location Diane Colress in Shady Sands
Base Size Medium
Amount of Containers 6
Parking Places 5
Other Notes -

Small House is medium sized base with one central building. Building is equipped with 6 containers: 2 bookshelves, crate, ice chest, desk and one nicely hidden tool desk. The outskirt of base are filled with cactuses and other extras making the whole map looks very nice. Parking has room for 5 automobiles. This base is quite cheap and looks gorgeous - if you can't afford more expensive bases pick this one.
