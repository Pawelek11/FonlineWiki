Power Armors

Here's list of very powerful armors, one's that practically not obtainable in the game. Well except Barett's Power Armor which is findable only in special encounter and stat's wise is on Combat Armor level.

Barett's Power Armor

This T51b armor is in a bad shape. The silver coating is worn out, and the armor is missing a Micro Fusion pack powering the hydraulic systems. Therefore, while offering decent protection, it hinders the user's movements due to its weight. Its previous owner painted it in some strange marking, making the armor look somewhat sinister.
AC bonus 10
Damage type Threshold Resistance
Normal 6 40
Laser 9 50
Fire 5 35
Plasma 5 30
Explode 9 45
Electrical 6 35
Anticrtical 0
Durability 200%
Weight 38555 grams
Base Price 9000 caps
Where to find? *Small Village
Notes The "Almost Power Armor" is very rare. True its deliver protection on level of Combat Armor Mark II but it's also really awesome!
