Description

This place looks like some kind of Deves Hidden Cottage, perhaps Skycast's Dacha? There is no regular way getting in here, there are few containers but i never menaged to check them all out, in the one i founded there were:

  • 20 Crates of Beers.
  • 7 Bootles of Booze.
  • 13 Never used sets of Condoms.
  • Music Box from Special Encounter.
  • Spare Green Robes.

and offcourse:

  • 2 Bozars for Aco.

When Deves aren't busy banning people, making updates or watching on uber sneak ongoing PvP battles they probably chill around this little place. Looks comfy!
