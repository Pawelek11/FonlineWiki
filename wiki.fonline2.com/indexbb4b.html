Madman in Vaultcity.

Descrition

In Vault City's Bar located on outskirt of town there's a certain man. Madman. He is looking for a Temple Key's. Everytime you bring one he will reward you with a bit of experience points. He accept up to 60-70 keys per one character.

Rewards

  • 600 experience point per each key.
