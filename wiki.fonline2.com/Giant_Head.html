Description

„
You encounter: A giant stone head standing in the clearing.
”

This somewhat familiar encounter (well if you played Fallout 2 that is) is a gateway to ultimate wisdom and of course in game experience. So you enter a dying forest encounter with nothing but trees...and off course a giant head in the middle.

Click on it, receive a piece of wisdom, click again receive another piece and so on. For first few clicks you will get 500 experience, then for another several clicks 1000 then after a little bit longer clicking session another 1500 experience points.
