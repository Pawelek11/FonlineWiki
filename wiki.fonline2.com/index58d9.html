Description

„
You encounter: A number of warehouses in a ruined city.
”

Khans Hideout is one of the most wanted special encounters in FOnline 2. It is also probably the most tough one. During your journey at some point of the game you may encounter something that looks like a forgotten warehouse. What seems to be an abandoned building soon enough turns out to be a small sized hideout inhabit by several mean and hostile Khan Raiders.

If you manage to clear out the hideout, you will be able to loot plenty of stuff including very rare item: G11 (It's rare tier 4 sub-machine small gun). The other loot located here seems to be spawned at random. While its always very good the more stuff become unlocked in global tech progression system, the better loot you can find here. Down below you can find an examplary loot which I managed to find in the end days of January 2015.

Playthrough

Depending on which mode you set your game while finding this encounter you will be fighting in either Turn based or Real time mode. In both of this cases expect very tough fight. This is one the encounters that cannot be done without bringing with you any gear (well unless you are very, very lucky or just a close combat fighter.)

Firstly after finding this encounter you are presented with two choices: A) Going open space or B) Going through the building. I personally recommend going through the building as having cover is quite a nice thing. Slowly penetrate the first building on the left, right next to place where you spawned. After its done your next objective is clearing the center warehouse (the one with "12" mark next to doors). This place is protected by locked doors but the NPC's might come from inside opening the doors in the process saving you troubles of lock picking it. Be sure to loot this room very thoroughly as this is the heart of this encounter. From this point you already located and taken the most important item in this place, the G11, you have now choice between going further and risking losing it or just leaving the location. If you feel strong enough you should continue as there is still plenty of lockers there which you haven't opened yet. Go through the small corridor to the top right building. It looks like this is where Khans Crafts their weaponry - which means, a lot of premium resources in the containers. Done? Great the last part of the complex awaits you. Go through the doors on the right and enter open space, fight Khans that will rush out from the last room. Then enter to southern room, where you can find even more top goodies.

Overall this whole location is being guarded by several Khans Raiders armed in good bursting weapons and big guns. Remember to proceed very carefully as each NPC here can deal significant amount of damage. This special encounter is not having any secrets. You can't get to any basement. You also can't get to several building on edge of the location. The lock mounted on that doors cannot be lock picked. Which means once you're done with looting the main complex your job here is done. Good work!
