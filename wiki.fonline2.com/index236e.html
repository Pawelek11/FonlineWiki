Laser Rifle (Ext. Cap.)

This Wattz 2000 laser rifle has had its recharging system upgraded and a special recycling chip installed that reduces the drain on the micro fusion cell by 50%. Min ST: 6.
Damage 40 - 60
Single AP: 5
Range 48
Damage type Laser
Ammo
Ammo capacity 24
Weapon Perk Long Range
Strength reqiuired 6
Item Durability 100
Weight 5443 grams
Base price 5000 caps
Exp for Crafting 200
Blueprint Recipe
Requirments to craft: 1x1x
1x]
Profession: Scientist 2
Notes Thanks to upgrading the clip, weapon somehow gains +2 hexes of range. Thanks to that its range is almost as good as Sniper Rifle making this weapon really good.
