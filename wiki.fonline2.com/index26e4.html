Description

FOnline 2 has an system of crafting based on professions. Every kind of job has 3 degrees, each unlocks new and improved items, and, assuming you also have found the blueprints, the possibility to craft them with bonus attributes.

The general know how and technical knowledge of producing, starting with small fire-arms, shotguns, all kinds of sub-machine guns, rifles, high grade, long range projectile weaponry and heavy arms.
