List of Tier 0 Energy Weapons

Laser Pistol

A Wattz 1000 Laser Pistol. Civilian model, so the wattage is lower than military or police versions. Uses small energy cells. Min ST: 3.
Damage 17 - 29
Single AP: 4
Range 28
Damage type Laser
Ammo
Ammo capacity 12
Weapon Perk None
Strength reqiuired 3
Item Durability 100
Weight 1814 grams
Base price 1000 caps
Exp for Crafting 160
Blueprint No
Requirments to craft: 2x1x
Energy Guns 60% or Profession: Scientist 1
Notes A Basic energy weapon. Its just something to start with.

Solar Scorcher

Without the sun's rays to charge this weapon's cpacitors this gun can't light a match. However, in full daylight, the experimental photo-electric cells that power the Scorcher allow it to turn almost anything into a crispy critter. Min ST: 3.
Damage 20 - 60
Single AP: 4
Range 20
Damage type Laser
Ammo Sunlight
Ammo capacity 6
Weapon Perk Accurate
Strength reqiuired 3
Item Durability 100
Weight 2267 grams
Base price 2000 caps
Where to find? Serra Controlling
Dungeons
Caves
Notes It can be recharged only at day, outdoor. Its uncommon but its quite easy to find. Overall its mostly a toy though.

Notes This weapon is root and core for entire Energy Weapon PvP class called Plasma Tank. Every Plasma Rifle has +8 Crit Roll bonus which can be futher increased by crafting better ones. So Plasma Rifle can have up to +18 on Crit Roll which is crazy good value.

YK32 Pulse Pistol

The YK32 is an electrical pulse weapon that was developed by the Yuma Flats Energy Consortium. Though powerful, the YK32 was never considered a practical weapon due to its inefficient energy usage and bulky design. Min ST: 3.

Strength reqiuired 3
Item Durability 100
Weight 2267 grams
Base price 3000 caps

Blueprint Yes
Requirments to craft: 3x3x1x

Notes This weapon is awesome in PvP since its omits nearly every armor in game(except for Tesla Armor, Brothood and Enclave combat armors). It has terrible range and very small magazine.
