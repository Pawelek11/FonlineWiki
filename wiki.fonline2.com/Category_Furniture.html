Description

Bored of the way your tent looks? Your base is not having enough thingies? Always wanted to buy a Nuka Cola Machine so you can store your Nukas? Now you can! Starting from session 3 in The Hub, Old Town you can find ghoul named Vince, he is a awesome furniture seller that can fulfill almost any furniture desire wastelander might have. From barrels through the chairs and beds to even elite Enclave President's Desk (don't ask how he got one) - that ghoul have it all. Vince accept only caps so don't hope on bringing him few useless stimpaks and then bartering them for extra useful stuff he deals in.

You can manipulate the visibility through Furniture by using repair skill on them (only applies to furniture that takes up to 1 hex of space) toggling seeing through furniture off might be handy when doing PvP arenas or similar stuff.

To place furniture just drop it on desired spot in your base/tent. To pick it up use steal skill (nope, no idea why steal skill is being used, but it works.) You can't place Furniture too close to vehicle spawn points. What you can do though is....

Furniture Fortress

Since only the tent owner/base menagers can pick off the furniture if you're worried about certain wastelanders scums taking stuff from you base (AKA "Base Rapers") you can place a closed circle of Furniture around your home. It won't look pretty but it's absolutely certain defense against any kind of thieves.

  • Misty's Base.

  • Unknow Base.

  • Rozpruwacz's Tent.

Note: Don't enter unknow bases that suddenly show up on your worldmap. It might be a dreadful furniture trap!

Note: If you ever get tricked into coming into such place and the base owner refuse to let you go, your only solution is suicide. Type ~cuttheveins and then you will be moved to respawn point. Any stuff you had on you while being caught will remain on the ground.

List of Furniture

There is 100 Furniture pieces in the Fonline 2. Each of them weight 4kg, some are cheaper, other more expensive. Please note that Vince won't have all 100 in offer, usually he will be having around 10 different furniture pieces, if you're searching for some exact furniture model you might have to check his shop several times till he spawn desired furniture...or buy it from other players.

The Furnitures doesn't have official names, they are segregated after serial number.
