Description

„
You encounter: An old hospital standing amongst the city ruins.
”

You could expect that after years of scavenging, the remnants of USA buildings or facility would be left untouched. Surprisingly enough those expectation are totally wrong as this special encounter shows up, that one facility has been not scavenged. You are lucky, real lucky you will be the first to unseal the doors of a long forgotten Clinic.

In this special encounter you won't find any enemies. Lock picking skills will be extremely handy though as while this place is not guarded by enemies its protected by many, many locks. In this article i will show examplary loot as from what i heard that this Hospital is spawning random loot every time a player encounter it.

This encounter is a throwback to 2238's server encounter with similar name. In that server it was famous for having an mysterious room - the only way to open it was to use crowbar or tools (the way was picked at random when player encountered this place sometimes it was just tools, sometimes just crowbar, sometimes even both at once) since having with you such items was really rare, the mysterious room remained mysterious for a long, long time. In the end some players managed to get in, just to find even more medical supplies. It was kinda of let down, you know?

As For FOnline 2 in this encounter there is no hidden rooms, no second levels. Its just plain one map with full of things to scavenge, so enjoy!
