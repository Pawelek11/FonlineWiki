Northern Robbers

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

A bit tougher version of Robbers that can be find at North of Fonline 2 world. They are still pretty easy to deal with for any half experienced wastelander with a gun and some armor.

Other Informations

Location: Northern arena of the map, on the Far Go caravan route.
Weaponry: Mausers, 10mm Pistols.
Extra Loot: Some caps and sometimes collectables.
Difficulty: Easy.
