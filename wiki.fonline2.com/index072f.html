Description

The Rippers are a gang initially from the Boneyard now residing in close zone to this city. Their Headquater used to be a Warehouse, but they were chased away by pack of certain cute not-so-little lizards (Hintwords: Clawes, Death, Lots of experience per kill). Other factions like Regulators, Blades and Gun Runners from Boneyards section are considering the Rippers to be their enemy/rivals in scavenging so its all out war in this region of the wasteland.

Other Informations

Location: Area near Boneyard.
Weaponry: Small Guns and Meele.
Extra Loot: Caps and minor loots.
Difficulty: Avarage.
