NCR Rangers

From FOnline 2 Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Description

NCR Rangers are the Republic elite army considering of many tough soldiers. Many of them are armored in custom made combat armors mk II called rangers armors. Those armors ain't lootable. NCR Rangers will not shoot innocent wastelanders, instead they engage with everyone with negative karma.

Other Informations

Location: Area near Shady Sands.
Weaponry: Some small guns and basic big guns.
Extra Loot: Nothing special caps and minor items.
Difficulty: A little tougher than average.
